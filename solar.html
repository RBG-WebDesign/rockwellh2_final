Solutions — Solar Pathway

Solar Energy In.
Hydrogen Power Out.

Our solar-to-hydrogen pathway uses renewable electricity from solar panels, wind, or hydro to split water into pure hydrogen through electrolysis. Clean, storable energy your facility can use on demand — for power, heat, or vehicle fuel.

How It Works

From Sunlight to Stored Power

A complete, closed-loop system — from renewable energy source to on-demand electricity or vehicle fuel.

1

Collect Renewable Energy

Solar panels, wind turbines, or hydroelectric systems generate electricity on-site or from the grid.

2

Electrolysis

An electrolyzer uses that electricity to split water (H₂O) into hydrogen gas and oxygen. No combustion, no carbon.

3

Hydrogen Storage

The hydrogen is compressed and stored in our Power Bank units at 350 bar (5,000 psi), ready for use anytime.

4

Power On Demand

When you need energy, hydrogen flows through a fuel cell that converts it back into electricity — or fuels your vehicles directly.
