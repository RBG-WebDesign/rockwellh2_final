How It Works

Three steps to on-site hydrogen.

01

Make hydrogen on site

Solar or biomass powers production — on your property, no utility connection needed.

02

Store your energy

Hydrogen tanks store energy for days — not hours. Always available on site.

03

Use it anytime

Clean electricity for your facility, fleet, or equipment. Zero emissions, no diesel, no grid bills.

Two Inputs. One System.

Most companies lock you into one energy source. We don't.

Rockwell H2 handles both solar electrolysis and biomass thermolysis. Both feed into the same storage and fuel cell system. You pick the input based on what your facility has. The hydrogen is the same either way.
